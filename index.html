Introduction

Howdy, My name is Audi !
I'm a college student of Information Systems, STMIK AMIKBANDUNG

I was born and also live in Bandung. My mother is from Jakarta, though.
I have a little sister, and she's 2 years younger than me, she's currently studying in Vocational High School.

I spend my leisure time mostly at home. I like listening to music. Listening to music is my new hobby. Emotion in music allows me to disconnect from my present reality, and reduce my anxiety. I might also gain new vocabularies through its lyrics. I cannot go a day without listening to music.

I'm a Software Engineering Graduate of SMK Negeri 4 Bandung. High School was a great experience with its own ups and downs. Though it started terribly, I'm glad it ended good. Though I made mistakes in High School which I might regret even now, I'm grateful it taught me valuable lessons. I used to hate High School Life a lot at the beginning, but at the end, I started to love High School Life which I miss a lot now.

I’m happy to step into college life, which provides more freedom and where, finally, I don’t have to come in a uniform. The reason why I chose STMIK "AMIKBANDUNG" is because I was offered financial assistance and it also doesn’t necessarily involve several years of full-time study in one place. Flexible study options let me choose how and when I learn.

Since I love listening things and almost everything, I've kinda become obsessed with voice. I wanted to become a voice actor or even a singer? I'm not sure if I'm aiming too high or too low, I don’t know if my dream is something I can really achieve or if I'm destined to fail. But my main purpose is to get better every single day. Keep focus on improving ourselves, our mind is everything, what we think, we become.
